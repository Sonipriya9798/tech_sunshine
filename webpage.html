Placement

The Training and placement cell of Purnea College of Engineering is headed by Prof. Saurabh Kumar. The aim and objective of this cell is to take care of the students’ training programme during the college days and guide them for seeking employment by arranging campus interviews in the college, make them aware of vacancies exiting in various industries/organizations and help them to achieve employment after passing out this institute. This cell is also responsible to organise the annual placement exercise of the institute and facilitates companies to conduct their recruiting procedure.

Campus Interviews

Companies will visit to newly established PCE, Purnea campus for recruitment of final year student of all branches.

Job Announcement Form

Companies/Recruiters who wish to conduct placement at the PCE, Purnea are requested to fill up the Job Announcement Form. It will help us to know your exact requirements and details of placement procedure and make adequate arrangements for your visit. Even if you wish to conduct off-campus interview, please let us know it by filling the form as we may consider to send our students to your location for recruitment process, if required.
